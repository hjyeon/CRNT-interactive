Introduction to ODE and its network architecture

ODE or System of ODEs is a simple predictive model with well-known solvers that survived the test of time. It does not suffer from problems like well-posedness and is easy to craft and put as explicit formulas, compare to models like PDEs. Furthermore, there is a simple graphical network that can provide a deep insight into the model. One can architect the network to get a model, similar to the relationship between NN and its underlying archtecture. Recently, people discovered that its parameters can be learned from large data.
